Unometry

A framework for Unifying General Relativity and Quantum Gravity

The Yield Constant ($Y$)

In this framework, $Y$ represents the efficiency of energy conversion within a specified spatial volume.

$$ Y = \frac{E}{V} $$

Velocity Yield Exceeded ($V_{ye}$)

When the Energy-to-Volume ratio reaches a critical threshold ($Y_{crit}$), the system enters the $V_{ye}$ state. This phase transition marks the point where velocity exceeds standard vacuum constraints.

$$ \text{Tach One Threshold: } Y > \Omega_{vac} $$
